LOPERENA

How was this name created?

The Loperena surname is a habitatiual name, meaning it was to people based on where they lived. In this case, Loperena likely comes from a property, house, or land called "Loperena" In Northern Spain. People who lived in on or owned that land reffered to as "de loperena", which simply just means "from Loperena". Over time, the "de" was dropped and the name became "Loperena".
